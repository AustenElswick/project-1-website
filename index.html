Black Metal

So you like metal?

But is black metal for you? Black metal is a small sub genre of metal music. Black metal is characterized by a couple things. First it is always played in a minor key. Secondly it has to be anti-christian. usually this means it's satanic, but many of the original black metal bands were not.

Some original bands.

  • venom
  • bathory
  • celtic frost
  • mercyful fate
  • hellhammer

The second wave

The second wave was spearheaded by a Norwegian movement. The second wave began in the 1990s and a lot of those bands still play today. the second wave was where the musical idea really became a concrete genre. some of the bands who formed the second wave are:

  • Gorgoroth
  • Mayhem
  • Darkthrone
  • Carpathian Forest
  • Burzum
